Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Health Data Collection

This application requests access to your health and fitness data through Google Health Connect to provide you with activity tracking features. We collect the following types of health data:

  • Steps: Daily step count to track your walking activity
  • Distance: Distance traveled during physical activities
  • Calories: Active calories burned during activities

How We Use Your Data

Your health data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your daily activity progress
  • Track your fitness goals and streaks
  • Show your position on the leaderboard (if you opt-in)
  • Provide personalized activity insights

Data Storage & Security

Your health data is:

  • Stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Associated with your user account for personalization
  • Synced to our secure servers to enable cross-device access
  • Retained only as long as your account is active

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your health data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your data may only be shared:

  • On the leaderboard (only if you explicitly enable this feature)
  • In aggregated, anonymized form for app improvement
  • When required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke health data permissions at any time through Health Connect settings
  • Request deletion of your stored health data
  • Opt-out of leaderboard visibility
  • Export your health data

Revoking Permissions

To revoke this app's access to your health data:

  1. Open the Health Connect app on your device
  2. Go to "App permissions"
  3. Find this app and tap it
  4. Revoke the permissions you no longer wish to grant

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your health data, please contact us through the app's support feature.
